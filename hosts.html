About Ayu

Ayu Lestari is a passionate cultural guide from Ubud, Bali, dedicated to sharing the rich traditions and spiritual essence of her homeland. With a deep understanding of Balinese customs and rituals, Ayu offers immersive experiences that connect travelers with the heart of Balinese culture. Her warm and engaging personality creates a welcoming atmosphere for visitors to learn and appreciate the beauty of Bali's heritage.
